WAN-TING KO

I am passionate about learning from people's stories and building meaningful interactions for a better experience.

MS in UX Research and Design @ University of Michigan

I am currently pursuing my Master’s degree in User Experience Design and Research at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. In the past two years, I taught English in both junior and senior divisions of the Affiliated Senior High School of National Taiwan Normal University.

Coming from a background in linguistics and education, I am trained to acutely observe where and when learners encounter difficulties and how I can design activities to facilitate communication and collaboration between learners. The two-year experience in education has made me an active listener and prepared me for communication skills to work in teams.

The best part I love about UX design and research is that, like teaching, I get to observe people’s needs from different angles and transform that to experience that allows users to complete tasks effortlessly while having a sense of accomplishment. I look forward to solving challenging problems by carrying out designs that help people achieve their goals.
